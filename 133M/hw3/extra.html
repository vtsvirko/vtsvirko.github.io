Extra-WURFL

Three capabilities offered by WURFL database
brand_name
A string that indicates the brand name of the device. (Nokia, Apple)
wireless_device
A Boolean that indicates whether the device is considered a wireless device.(True, False)
is_tablet
A Boolean that indicates whether the device is a tablet. This is an OS-independent description. (True, False)
